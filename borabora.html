Mt. Otemanu Cave

Mt. Otemanu has a beautiful cave around the summit. The cave has a breathtaking overlook of Bora Bora. The climb to the cave takes around 2 to 2.5 hours. When you reach the cave, you may find the graves of ancestors and nesting birds. This is a mind-blowing experience that we would love to partake in.
