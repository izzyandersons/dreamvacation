Australia

Lion Island

Lion Island, also known as Sea Lion Island is an Island Nature reserve. This island is home to wildlife, including sea lions and penguins. There is a small private hotel on the island for visitors to come see the beauty of the island. All fences have been removed to give the wildlife easier access to all parts of the island. This is a beautiful island that we would love to visit.
